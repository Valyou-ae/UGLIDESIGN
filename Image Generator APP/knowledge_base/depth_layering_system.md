## ðŸŽ¯ Core Techniques

### Foreground Elements

**Essential Keywords:**  
`foreground detail`, `near elements`, `depth framing`, `foreground interest`

**Technical Definition:**  
Closest layer to camera, often used for framing and depth

**Characteristics:**  
Sharpest detail, Framing potential, Depth creation, Leading lines

**Elements:**  
Frame elements, Leading lines, Focus points, Anchors

**Best Use Cases:**  
Depth creation, Natural frames, Interest points, Composition anchors

---

### Midground Subject

**Essential Keywords:**  
`midground focus`, `subject placement`, `main interest`, `focal point`

**Technical Definition:**  
Middle distance where main subject typically resides

**Characteristics:**  
Primary focus, Sharpest detail, Main subject, Action occurs

**Best Use Cases:**  
Subject placement, Main action, Focal interest, Story center

---

### Background Elements

**Essential Keywords:**  
`background depth`, `distant elements`, `context layer`, `atmospheric background`

**Technical Definition:**  
Furthest layer providing context and depth

**Characteristics:**  
Context setting, Atmospheric, Reduced detail, Depth cues

**Elements:**  
Environmental context, Sky, Distant features, Atmosphere

**Best Use Cases:**  
Context setting, Atmosphere, Depth enhancement, Scene establishment

---

### Layer Separation Methods

**Methods:**

- **Dof:** Shallow depth of field - blur separation
- **Atmospheric:** Haze and fog - distance separation
- **Lighting:** Differential lighting - light/shadow separation
- **Color:** Color temperature - warm foreground, cool background
- **Contrast:** Contrast gradient - high near, low far
- **Scale:** Size difference - large near, small far
