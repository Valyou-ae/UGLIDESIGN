## ðŸŽ¯ Core Techniques

### Rule of Thirds

**Essential Keywords:**  
`rule of thirds`, `thirds composition`, `power points`, `grid composition`

**Technical Definition:**  
Divide frame into 9 equal parts with 2 horizontal and 2 vertical lines

**Placement:**  
Subject on intersections, Horizon on horizontal line, Key elements on gridlines

**Best Use Cases:**  
Balanced composition, Dynamic placement, Professional framing

---

### Golden Ratio / Fibonacci

**Essential Keywords:**  
`golden ratio`, `fibonacci spiral`, `phi grid`, `divine proportion`

**Technical Definition:**  
1:1.618 ratio creating naturally pleasing composition

**Applications:**  
Spiral composition, Golden grid, Natural flow, Harmonious balance

**Best Use Cases:**  
Artistic composition, Natural aesthetics, Pleasing proportions

---

### Leading Lines

**Essential Keywords:**  
`leading lines`, `compositional lines`, `visual flow`, `directional lines`

**Technical Definition:**  
Lines that guide viewer's eye through the composition

**Types:**  
Diagonal, Curved, Converging, Parallel

**Best Use Cases:**  
Depth creation, Eye guidance, Dynamic composition

---

### Natural Framing

**Essential Keywords:**  
`natural frame`, `frame within frame`, `foreground framing`, `compositional frame`

**Technical Definition:**  
Using elements within scene to frame the subject

**Elements:**  
Doorways, Windows, Arches, Trees, Architectural elements

**Best Use Cases:**  
Focus attention, Depth creation, Context setting

---

### Symmetrical Composition

**Essential Keywords:**  
`symmetrical`, `balanced composition`, `mirror composition`, `centered`

**Technical Definition:**  
Equal visual weight on both sides of frame

**Types:**  
Vertical symmetry, Horizontal symmetry, Radial symmetry

**Best Use Cases:**  
Formal compositions, Architecture, Portraits, Dramatic impact

---

### Negative Space

**Essential Keywords:**  
`negative space`, `minimalist composition`, `breathing room`, `isolation`

**Technical Definition:**  
Empty space around subject creating emphasis and breathing room

**Effects:**  
Subject isolation, Minimalist feel, Focus attention, Modern aesthetic

**Best Use Cases:**  
Minimalist design, Product photography, Artistic shots, Clean aesthetic

---

### Depth Plane Composition

**Essential Keywords:**  
`depth planes`, `layered composition`, `foreground midground background`

**Technical Definition:**  
Distinct visual layers creating three-dimensional depth

**Layers:**  
Foreground interest, Midground subject, Background context

**Best Use Cases:**  
Cinematic depth, 3D feeling, Professional composition
