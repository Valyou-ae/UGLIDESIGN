# STYLE: Chinese Ink Painting

## Overview
Traditional East Asian art using brush and ink with emphasis on spontaneity and natural subjects.

## Key Characteristics
- Monochromatic ink washes
- Visible brush strokes
- Emphasis on empty space
- Natural subjects (bamboo, mountains, birds)
- Calligraphic quality
- Gradations from wet to dry brush

## Essential Keywords
`Chinese ink painting`, `shuimo`, `brush painting`, `East Asian ink art`, `Song dynasty style`

## Prompt Templates

**Professional:**
"[subject] in traditional Chinese ink painting style, monochromatic black ink on rice paper, visible spontaneous brush strokes, emphasis on negative space, misty atmospheric depth, calligraphic line quality, gradations from wet to dry brush, Song dynasty aesthetic"

## Technical Parameters
- Medium: Black ink on white/cream paper
- Brush Work: Visible, expressive strokes
- Composition: Asymmetrical with abundant space
- Values: Range from pale gray to deep black
