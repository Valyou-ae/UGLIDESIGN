# STYLE 115: BOTANICAL ILLUSTRATION

## Overview
Scientific plant illustration with meticulous accuracy for botanical identification and documentation, combining natural history tradition with artistic beauty.

## Key Characteristics
- **Precise Botanical Accuracy**: Species identification level
- **Multiple Views**: Whole plant, flower detail, cross-sections
- **Watercolor Technique**: Traditional scientific illustration
- **White or Cream Backgrounds**: Clean presentation
- **Labels and Annotations**: Scientific nomenclature
- **Natural Color Accuracy**: True-to-life pigments
- **Detail Levels**: From habit to cellular
- **Flat Lighting**: Even, non-dramatic

## Essential Keywords
`botanical illustration`, `scientific plant drawing`, `botanical art`, `natural history illustration`, `flora illustration`, `plant specimen art`, `botanical watercolor`, `herbarium art`

## Technical Parameters
- **Accuracy**: Botanically verified, species-level ID
- **Views**: Habit, details, sections, life stages
- **Media**: Watercolor, ink, colored pencil
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF), Cream (#FFF8DC)
- **Scale**: Noted for size reference

## Color Palette
- **Greens**: Accurate chlorophyll tones (#228B22, #6B8E23, #556B2F)
- **Flowers**: True natural colors
- **Browns**: Realistic bark, soil (#8B4513, #A0522D)
- **Muted**: Natural, not enhanced

## Best Use Cases
- Field guides, Botanical journals, Herbarium records, Gardening books, Natural history museums, Species documentation, Educational materials, Conservation records
