## ðŸŽ¯ Core Techniques

### Three-Point Lighting

**Essential Keywords:**  
`three-point lighting`, `key light`, `fill light`, `rim light`, `studio lighting`

**Technical Definition:**  
Classic setup with key (main), fill (shadow reducer), and back/rim (separation) lights

**Ratios:**

- **Low Contrast:** 2:1 ratio, soft and even
- **Standard:** 4:1 ratio, moderate contrast
- **Dramatic:** 8:1 ratio, strong shadows
- **Chiaroscuro:** 16:1+ ratio, extreme contrast

**Best Use Cases:**  
Portraits, Product photography, Interviews, Studio shots

---

### Butterfly Lighting

**Essential Keywords:**  
`butterfly lighting`, `paramount lighting`, `beauty lighting`, `top-down light`

**Technical Definition:**  
Light positioned directly above and in front, creating butterfly shadow under nose

**Characteristics:**  
Symmetrical shadows, Glamorous look, Emphasizes cheekbones, Minimizes blemishes

**Best Use Cases:**  
Beauty photography, Fashion, Glamour shots, Commercial

### Practical Lighting

**Essential Keywords:**  
`practical lights`, `in-scene lighting`, `motivated lighting`, `diegetic light`

**Technical Definition:**  
Visible light sources within the scene (lamps, windows, fire, etc.)

**Types:**  
Table lamps, Street lights, Windows, Fire, Neon signs, Candles

**Best Use Cases:**  
Realistic scenes, Environmental lighting, Atmospheric shots, Narrative lighting
