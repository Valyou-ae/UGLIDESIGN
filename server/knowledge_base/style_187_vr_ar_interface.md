# STYLE 187: VR/AR INTERFACE

## Historical Fashion Foundations
Primary Eras: Space Age (1960s), Cyberpunk (1980s-90s), Techwear (2010s-present), Augmented Couture (2020s)

## Core Style DNA
- **Floating Elements**: Pierre Cardin's 1960s architectural pieces that "defied gravity"
- **Transparency**: 1990s sheer fabrics, PVC fashion, holographic materials
- **Spatial Awareness**: Kimono's ma (negative space), Issey Miyake's body-space relationship
- **Gestural Interface**: Dance/performance costume's movement vocabulary

## Color Theory & Palette
### Holographic Primary:
- Cyan (#00FFFF) - high visibility
- Magenta (#FF00FF) - accent/warning
- Electric blue (#7DF9FF) - neutral UI
- Neon green (#39FF14) - confirmation
### Transparency Gradients:
- 0% opacity: invisible at rest
- 20-40%: ambient awareness
- 60-80%: active selection
- 100%: critical information
### Depth Coding:
- Near field: warm hues (red, orange)
- Mid field: neutral (white, blue)
- Far field: cool receding (deep blue, purple)

## Material Language
### Glass & Acrylic:
- Frosted UI panels (like 1960s Lucite furniture)
- Refractive edges (crystal facets)
- Fresnel effects on viewing angle
### Light as Fabric:
- Volumetric rays (like stage lighting through sheer curtains)
- Caustics and light scatter
- Emissive threads (fiber optic couture)
### Energy Fields:
- Force field shimmer (soap bubble iridescence)
- Particle systems as embroidery
- Electromagnetic "weave"

## Design Principles
- **Comfortable Eye Distance**: 0.5m-infinity focal range (like reading glasses to distance vision)
- **Gestural Elegance**: Movements should feel like T'ai Chi, not labor
- **Spatial Hierarchy**: Important = closer, secondary = periphery
- **Fade-In Philosophy**: Elements appear when needed, vanish when not (like couture reveals)

## Modern Implementation
### Unity/Unreal VR UI Best Practices:
- Canvas distance: 1.5-3 meters
- Text size: minimum 24pt at 2m
- Button size: 40mm+ for reliable selection
- Hover state: 0.3s delay before activation
- Confirmation: always for destructive actions
### AR Specific:
- Plane detection for surface mounting
- Environmental light matching
- Occlusion mapping for realism
- Anchoring stability (no UI drift)
### Accessibility:
- Voice control backup
- Adjustable UI scale
- High contrast mode
- Motion sickness considerations (no forced camera movement)
