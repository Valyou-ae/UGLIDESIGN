# 180. DISNEY 2D ANIMATION

### **Core Characteristics:**
- **Hand-Drawn**: Traditional cel animation (or digital equivalent)
- **Principles**: Disney's 12 principles mastered
- **Fluid Motion**: 24 fps on "ones" or "twos"
- **Squash & Stretch**: Extreme deformation for expression
- **Theatrical**: Broad, readable from distance

### **Traditional Animation Process:**

**Production Pipeline (Classic):**
```
1. Story/Script:
   - Storyboards (key moments)
   - Visual narrative

2. Character Design:
   - Model sheets
   - Turnarounds
   - Expression sheets
   - Color keys

3. Layout:
   - Background design
   - Camera placement
   - Staging

4. Animation:
   - Rough animation (key poses)
   - Clean-up (final lines)
   - In-betweening

5. Backgrounds:
   - Hand-painted
   - Watercolor or gouache
   - Atmospheric depth

6. Ink & Paint:
   - Cel painting
   - Color separation
   - Registration

7. Photography:
   - Cel over background
   - Camera exposure
   - Composite

8. Post-Production:
   - Sound sync
   - Music
   - Final composite
```

### **Disney Animation Eras:**

**Golden Age (1937-1942):**
```
Films:
- Snow White (1937): First feature
- Pinocchio (1940): Peak craftsmanship
- Fantasia (1940): Artistic ambition
- Dumbo (1941): Simple, emotional
- Bambi (1942): Realistic animals

Style:
- Rotoscoped reference (realistic movement)
- Detailed backgrounds
- Multiplane camera (depth)
- Rich colors
- European fairy tale aesthetic
```

**Renaissance (1989-1999):**
```
Films:
- The Little Mermaid (1989)
- Beauty and the Beast (1991)
- Aladdin (1992)
- The Lion King (1994)
- Mulan (1998)

Revolution:
- Broadway musical influence
- Computer-assisted animation (CAPS)
- Detailed, lush backgrounds
- Complex camera moves
- Golden age return
```

### **Character Design Philosophy:**

**Appeal & Silhouette:**
```
Readable Shapes:
- Clear silhouette (recognizable in black)
- Simple primary shapes
- Avoid symmetry (more dynamic)
- Distinct proportions
```

**Villains:**
```
- Sharp angles
- Thin, elongated (Maleficent, Jafar)
- OR large, imposing (Ursula, Gaston)
- Cold eyes, small pupils
- Dramatic gestures
```
